Core Team Members
Michele Focchi
Team Leader
Michele Focchi is a leading expert in motion planning and control of quadruped robots, and team leader of this project, with 16 years of experience in robotics and pioneering contributions to heuristic locomotion in unstructured terrains. His research combines control, optimization, and machine learning to improve quadruped performance in challenging environments. Currently a Professor at the University of Trento (DISI) and Scientific Advisor for All3, he was also a visiting scientist at IIT, where he co-founded the Dynamic Legged Systems Lab.
